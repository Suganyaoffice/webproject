Our RCM Process

1. Patient Registration

Accurate demographic and insurance data entry for faster claims processing.

2. Eligibility Verification

We verify benefits and insurance before appointments to reduce denials.

3. Medical Coding

Expert coding with ICD-10, CPT, and HCPCS for proper claim classification.

4. Charge Entry & Submission

We capture all billable services and submit clean claims to payers.

5. Payment Posting

Automated ERA posting with accurate reconciliation and adjustments.

6. AR Follow-up

Dedicated AR team works on unpaid claims and rejections.

7. Denial Management

We analyze and resolve denials quickly to recover revenue.
